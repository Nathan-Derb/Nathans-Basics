Why do people choose cats?

  • More dependent, they tend to sleep most the day.
  • They live long live's and are healthy animals.
  • They take up less space "not compared to teacup dogs" which is convenient for apartments.
  • They're easier to potty train, often needing no training at all.
  • Cat's are good for pest control killing insects and small rodents.

Why do people choose dogs?

  • Dog's help promote a more active life
  • They are amazing for security, protective during walks and a deturrent for home intuders.
  • With some effort dogs can learn an astonishing amount of tricks.
  • Dog's can help teach kids with self esteem, compassion, and empathy.
  • Above all dogs are known for their loyalty.
